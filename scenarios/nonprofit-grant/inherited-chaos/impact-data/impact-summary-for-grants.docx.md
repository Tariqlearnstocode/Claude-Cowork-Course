# Detroit Youth Arts - Impact Summary FY25-26

*Prepared by Marcus Williams for grant applications*

---

## YOUTH SERVED

**Total Youth Served (FY25):** 312
**Projected Youth Served (FY26):** 350

### Breakdown by Program:
- After-School Arts Academy: 185 youth
- Summer Arts Intensive: 72 youth
- Saturday Studios: 85 youth (estimated)
- Teen Leadership Program: 18 youth

*Note: Some youth participate in multiple programs; figures reflect unique participants with minimal overlap.*

---

## RETENTION & COMPLETION

**After-School Retention Rate:** 94%
**Summer Intensive Completion Rate:** 91%
**Teen Leadership Completion Rate:** 100%

---

## OUTCOME DATA

### Academic Improvement
- **87%** of regular participants showed improvement in at least one academic subject
- **92%** of participants maintained or improved school attendance
- **3.2 GPA** average among Teen Leadership participants

### Social-Emotional Development
- **94%** of participants report increased confidence in their creative abilities
- **89%** of participants report feeling "more connected" to their community
- **78%** of parents report positive behavioral changes at home

### Arts Skill Development
- **96%** of participants demonstrated measurable improvement in at least one art form
- **23** student works selected for external exhibitions
- **7** students received awards in regional competitions

### Long-Term Outcomes
- **100%** of Teen Leadership alumni graduated high school (past 3 cohorts)
- **5** alumni received full or partial arts scholarships to college
- **3** alumni are now employed as teaching artists with DYA
