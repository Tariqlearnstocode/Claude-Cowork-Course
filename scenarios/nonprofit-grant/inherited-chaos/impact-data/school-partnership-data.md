## SCHOOL OUTCOMES (Where Tracked)

### Attendance Impact
*Data from 2 schools that shared records (Clippert, DIA)*

Students attending DYA 2+ days/week (n=42):
- School attendance rate: 91% (vs. 84% school average)
- Fewer behavioral referrals: 67% had zero referrals

*Note: Self-selected sample. Students who come to DYA regularly may already be more engaged.*

### Teacher Feedback
Surveyed: 12 teachers across partner schools
Response rate: 8 teachers (67%)

"Have you noticed changes in DYA students?"
- More confident in class: 6/8
- More engaged in learning: 5/8
- Better peer relationships: 4/8
- Improved grades: 3/8
- No noticeable change: 2/8

Selected comments:
- "Jaylen is a different kid since he started at DYA. He actually raises his hand now."
- "I wish more of my students could attend."
- "The art skills definitely transfer - Amara's science diagrams are beautiful now."
