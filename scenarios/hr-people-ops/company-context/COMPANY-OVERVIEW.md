## Products

### BuildFlow Pro (Launched 2023)

Core project management platform for construction companies.

**Features:**
- Contractor scheduling and coordination
- Document management (specs, drawings, contracts)
- Real-time communication and notifications
- Mobile app for field workers
- Reporting and analytics dashboard
- Integrations: QuickBooks, Procore, PlanGrid, Autodesk

**Pricing:**
- Starter: $299/month (up to 5 active projects)
- Professional: $599/month (up to 25 active projects)
- Enterprise: $1,499/month (unlimited projects, dedicated support)

### BuildFlow Safety (Launched Q3 2025)

Safety compliance add-on module.

**Features:**
- Safety compliance tracking
- Incident reporting and investigation
- OSHA documentation and recordkeeping
- Training record management
- Safety audit checklists

**Pricing:**
- $99/month add-on to any BuildFlow Pro plan
