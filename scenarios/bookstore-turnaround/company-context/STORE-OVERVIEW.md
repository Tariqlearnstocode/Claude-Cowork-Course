# Inkwell Books - Store Overview

## Location & Facility

**Address:** 2847 Michigan Avenue, Corktown, Detroit MI 48216

**Neighborhood Context:**
Corktown is Detroit's oldest neighborhood, experiencing significant gentrification since 2015. Mix of young professionals, longtime residents, artists, and families. High foot traffic on weekends, moderate weekdays. Several restaurants, coffee shops, and boutiques nearby.

**Building:**
- Main floor: 3,200 sq ft
- Basement: 800 sq ft (used for storage and small events)
- Street-facing windows: 24 linear feet
- Parking: Street only, can be challenging on weekends
- Accessibility: One step at entrance (not ADA compliant - Eleanor never addressed this)

**Lease Terms:**
- Monthly rent: $4,200 (significantly below market rate of ~$6,500)
- Lease expires: June 2026 (18 months)
- Landlord has indicated rent will increase to market rate at renewal
- Building sale would terminate lease with 90-day notice
