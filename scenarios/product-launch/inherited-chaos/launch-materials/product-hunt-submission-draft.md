## Why I'm not recommending we do Product Hunt

I drafted this submission, but I don't think we should launch on Product Hunt right now. Here's why:

1. **Product isn't ready.** Mobile app is slow, onboarding is confusing, we're missing key features. PH users are ruthlessâ€”they'll call us out.

2. **We don't know our positioning.** "Scheduling for everyone" won't stand out. PH rewards specific, novel products.

3. **Can't handle the traffic.** If we get featured, we'll get thousands of visitors. Our support is one person. Our servers haven't been load-tested properly.

4. **Negative reviews are permanent.** A bad PH launch follows you forever. Better to wait until we're ready.

My recommendation: Launch on PH in Q1 2025 after we fix the core issues. But Dana wants to do it at launch, so here's the draft.
