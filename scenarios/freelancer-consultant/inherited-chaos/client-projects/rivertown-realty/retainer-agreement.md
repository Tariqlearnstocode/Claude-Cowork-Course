# Retainer Agreement: Rivertown Realty Group
## Digital Marketing Services

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2024
**Client:** Rivertown Realty Group
**Contact:** David Nguyen, Principal Broker
**Provider:** Corktown Creative Collective

---

## Scope of Services

Corktown Creative will provide ongoing digital marketing services including:

### Social Media Management
- Content strategy and calendar development
- Creation of 3 social media posts per week (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- Community engagement (responding to comments, basic monitoring)
- Monthly performance reporting

### Content Marketing
- 1 blog post per month (500-800 words)
- SEO optimization for blog content
- Content topic development aligned with marketing goals

### Strategic Support
- Quarterly strategy review meetings
- Ongoing consultation on digital marketing opportunities
- Coordination with other marketing efforts as needed

---

## Monthly Investment

**Retainer Fee:** $3,000 per month
**Payment Terms:** Due on the 1st of each month

---

## Term

Initial term: 6 months (July 1, 2024 - December 31, 2024)
Auto-renewal: Month-to-month after initial term
Cancellation: 30 days written notice by either party

---

## Deliverables Schedule

**Weekly:**
- 3 social posts (drafted, scheduled, or posted depending on approval workflow)

**Monthly:**
- 1 blog post
- Social media performance report
- Brief check-in call or email

**Quarterly:**
- Strategy review meeting (60-90 minutes)
- Quarterly performance summary
- Updated recommendations

---

## Client Responsibilities

- Provide timely feedback on content (within 48 hours)
- Share company news, listings, and updates for content
- Provide access to necessary accounts and platforms
- Attend scheduled meetings and check-ins
- Communicate goals and priorities

---

## What's Not Included

- Paid advertising (management or spend)
- Website development or maintenance
- Video production
- Photography
- Print materials
- Additional blog posts or social campaigns

*Additional services available at standard hourly rate ($150/hour)*

---

## Success Metrics

We will track and report on:
- Social media follower growth
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Website traffic from social and blog
- Lead generation indicators
