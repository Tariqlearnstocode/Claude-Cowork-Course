# Project Agreement: Detroit Athletics Club
## Rebrand & Expansion Strategy

**Client:** Detroit Athletics Club
**Contact:** Tanya Washington, Marketing Director
**Project Lead:** Jordan Reeves, Corktown Creative
**Date:** November 1, 2024

---

## Background

Detroit Athletics Club is a locally-owned gym chain operating two locations (Midtown and Royal Oak). They are expanding to four locations (adding Ferndale and Downtown) in Q2 2025 and need updated brand strategy and identity to support the growth.

---

## Project Scope

### Phase 1: Brand Audit & Research (Complete)
- Current brand assessment
- Member feedback synthesis
- Competitive landscape analysis
- Stakeholder interviews

### Phase 2: Brand Strategy (In Progress)
- Brand positioning and differentiation
- Brand architecture (multi-location strategy)
- Messaging framework
- Visual direction exploration

### Phase 3: Board Presentation (Due Jan 27)
- Executive summary of brand strategy
- Recommended visual direction
- Implementation roadmap
- Investment requirements

### Phase 4: Identity Development (Pending approval)
- Logo refinement
- Visual identity system
- Brand guidelines
- Location-specific adaptations
