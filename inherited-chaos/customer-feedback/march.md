- Server at Dearborn: "People seem more excited about our seasonal specials than the rewards program"
- Customer asked: "Is there any reason to hit the higher tiers?"

### App Usage Stats This Month

### Social Media Mentions

## Summary
Satisfaction trending slightly down. The "it's fine" sentiment continues. Notable feedback: people feel the program doesn't match State Fair's personality. One comment stuck with me: "I go to Applebee's for rewards, here for good food." That's... not great.
