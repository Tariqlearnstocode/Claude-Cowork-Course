# Competitor Research: Chili's

## Program Overview
**Name:** My Chili's Rewards
**Approach:** Simple value, free food focus
**Vibe:** Friendly, straightforward rewards

## How It Works
- Points per purchase
- Free chips & salsa on every visit (members only)
- Free dessert rewards
- Birthday reward
- But honestly, the free chips are the hook...

## What They Do Well

### 1. Immediate Value
- Free chips & salsa EVERY visit for members
- No points required, instant benefit
- Creates habit: "we're Chili's members, we get free chips"

### 2. Simple Structure
- Easy to understand
- No confusing tiers
- Clear path to free food
- No app required (can use phone number)

### 3. Family Focus
- Kid-friendly rewards
- Family meal deals for members
- Makes the whole family feel included

### 4. Consistency
- Same experience every location
- Staff trained to mention membership
- Reliable rewards delivery

## Their Weaknesses
- Not very exciting or memorable
- Rewards program itself is basic
- No real personalization
- Doesn't create "super fans"

## What We Can Learn
- Immediate value beats eventual rewards
- Simple > complicated
- Create a ritual (their chips = reason to mention membership every visit)
- Make it easy to participate

## Key Insight
Chili's doesn't win on sophistication - they win on SIMPLICITY and IMMEDIATE VALUE. Free chips on every visit gives people a reason to scan every single time.

Quote from their approach: "We make it easy to feel rewarded."

## The Big Takeaway
You can build loyalty with a simple program if you nail immediate value. Chili's proves that one reliable benefit (free chips) can be more powerful than complicated points systems.

What's our "free chips"? What can we give people EVERY visit?
